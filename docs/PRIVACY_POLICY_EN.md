# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 17, 2025**

## Introduction

Methodica ("we", "our app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection and Use

**Currently, Methodica does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.**

The application operates exclusively on your local device and does not send any information to external servers.

## Local Data

All data you create or enter in the app is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible to us or third parties.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use services provided by Apple (for iOS devices) for basic operating system functionality, which are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy available at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

## Push Notifications

If you use push notifications, these are managed through Apple's notification services (APNs). We do not collect or track the contents of notifications. For more information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

## Future Changes

We reserve the right to introduce new features that may involve data collection in the future. In such cases:
- We will update this privacy policy
- We will notify you of changes through an in-app notice
- We will request your explicit consent before collecting any personal data

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no need to request access, modification, or deletion of data.

If we collect data in the future, you will have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to the processing of your data

## Data Security

Although we do not collect data, we are committed to maintaining the security of the app and protecting the integrity of your local data.
